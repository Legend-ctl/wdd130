Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant country located in West Africa, known for its diverse culture, rich history, and vast natural resources. It is the most populous country in Africa, with over 200 million people and more than 250 ethnic groups. Nigeria is famous for its music, fashion, and Nollywood film industry, which is one of the largest in the world. The country has varied landscapes, from the beaches and mangroves in the south to the savannas and hills in the north. Despite facing challenges such as corruption and infrastructure development, Nigeria remains a nation of resilient people, economic potential, and cultural pride.
